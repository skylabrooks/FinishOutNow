## üí¨ Handling Objections

### **Objection #1: "How accurate is this AI?"**

**What They're Really Asking:** Can I trust this or will I waste time on bad leads?

**Your Answer:**
*"Great question. Our AI is 75-90% accurate on trade matches. But here's the key‚Äîwe verify the company against Texas Comptroller data. So if it says 'Verified Entity,' you know the company actually exists and is actively doing business. Plus, you get a free 2-week trial. Try 50-100 leads. If the quality isn't there, you stop. No risk."*

**If They Say:** "That doesn't sound good enough"
**Counter:** *"Most brokers don't verify at all. You're actually getting better intel. But you're right‚Äîthat's why we do the free trial. See for yourself."*

---

### **Objection #2: "We already have a broker / lead source"**

**What They're Really Asking:** Why would I switch or pay for both?

**Your Answer:**
*"Actually, most of our customers use brokers AND us. Here's why: Brokers react after the fact. We're proactive‚Äîyou see permits the DAY they're filed, sometimes days before brokers' lists go out. So you reach out first. Plus, you're not paying per-lead fees‚Äîit's one flat monthly rate. Use us to find more leads, keep your broker relationship for big deals. Make sense?"*

**If They Ask:** "How much does it cost?"
**Answer:** *"[Price]. Unlimited leads in 5 cities. No per-lead fees, no surprises. We can try it for 2 weeks free first."*

---

### **Objection #3: "We don't have time to check a new system every day"**

**What They're Really Asking:** Will this create more work for my team?

**Your Answer:**
*"Actually, it saves time. Here's what our customers tell us: Instead of spending 2 hours researching leads on city websites, they spend 20 minutes on Early BookS. The AI already did the research. They just need to decide 'Do I want this lead or not?' and claim it. That's it."*

**If They Say:** "But we'd have to train our team"
**Counter:** *"5-minute setup. Seriously. I'll do it on a Zoom call or phone. Your team can start using it immediately. We also have 24/7 chat support if they get stuck."*

---

### **Objection #4: "This sounds too good to be true"**

**What They're Really Asking:** Is there a catch?

**Your Answer:**
*"The only 'catch' is that you have to actually reach out to the leads. Early BookS finds them, verifies them, and scores them. But closing is up to you. That said, because you're reaching out first, your odds are way better than if you waited weeks. Plus, 2-week trial. If it's not working, you know after 2 weeks. No commitment."*

---

### **Objection #5: "Your competitor does the same thing"**

**What They're Really Asking:** Why you over them?

**Your Answer:**
*"Couple reasons: First, [state specific advantage: faster updates / better AI / local focus / better interface]. Second, we have the claiming system‚Äîfirst contractor to claim gets it. That's unique. Third, we focus on [Security/Signage/IT] contractors specifically, not everyone. We're built for your business. Want to try us head-to-head? 2-week trial and you'll see."*

## üé¨ Closing Techniques

### **Technique #1: The Trial Close (BEST FOR NOVICES)**
*"Let's just get you started with a 2-week trial. No credit card, no commitment. You try it, see if the leads are good, and then we can talk about moving forward. Sound fair?"*

**Why it works:** Low pressure. They can't say no to "free."

---

### **Technique #2: The Alternative Close**
*"So, do you want to start with our [Plan A] or would [Plan B] work better for your team?"*

**Why it works:** Both options move them forward. They're just picking which one.

---

### **Technique #3: The Time Close**
*"I can get you set up in 5 minutes. Should we do it right now on this call, or do you want to try it tonight and I'll follow up tomorrow?"*

**Why it works:** Gives them control but either way you're getting them set up.

---

### **Technique #4: The Competitor Close**
*"Your competitors are probably already using something like this. Is that something you want to stay ahead of, or are you good where you're at?"*

**Why it works:** FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out). Works on competitive business owners.

---

### **Technique #5: The Question Close**
*"If I could guarantee you'd have 3-5 new qualified leads per week for the next month, would that be worth trying for 2 weeks?"*

**Why it works:** They answer yes to the question before they realize they just agreed.

## üéì Pro Tips for Novices

### **Tip #1: Believe in the Product**
If you don't think this is valuable, they won't either. Genuinely believe Early BookS saves contractors time and money. It shows.

### **Tip #2: Ask More Questions**
Don't talk the whole time. Ask about THEIR business first. Then explain how Early BookS helps THEM.

### **Tip #3: Use the Trial**
You don't have to close on a sale. Close on a trial. Trials convert way easier. Get them in, they'll see the value.

### **Tip #4: Follow Up Fast**
Send trial access within 1 hour of them saying yes. Set calendar reminders for day 3, day 7, day 10. Most people forget‚Äîyou won't.

### **Tip #5: Celebrate Wins**
First demo? Win. First trial? Win. First paid customer? HUGE win. Tell your manager. You earned it.
