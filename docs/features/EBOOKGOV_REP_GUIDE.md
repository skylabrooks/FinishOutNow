# E BookGov Appointment Setting - Quick Start Guide

## üéØ Your Role

As an E BookGov Appointment Setting Representative, you help our clients convert high-quality Commercial TI leads into scheduled appointments. You'll make up to 6 cold call attempts over 2 weeks to set meetings between contractors and prospects.

## üìã Daily Workflow

### 1. Check Your Assigned Leads

Look for leads with **"Email Sent"** status - these are ready for calling!

### 2. Review Lead Details

Before calling, review:
- **Business name** and **address**
- **Project type** (Security, Signage, Low-Voltage IT)
- **AI analysis** - confidence score, trade opportunities
- **Email template** the client sent (Email Tab)

### 3. Make Your Call

**Preparation:**
- Have lead details open
- Review the email that was sent
- Check previous call attempts (if any)

**During Call:**
- Be professional and concise
- Reference the email sent by the contractor
- Focus on value: efficiency, compliance, competitive pricing
- Goal: Schedule 15-minute discovery call

**Key Talking Points:**
- "Hi, I'm [Your Name] with E BookGov, following up on an email about your [project type] at [address]"
- "Our client specializes in [Security/Signage/IT] for commercial tenant improvements"
- "Would you be open to a brief 15-minute call to discuss?"

### 4. Record the Call Attempt

**Immediately after each call:**

1. Click **"Set Appointment"** on the lead
2. Go to **"Call Tracking"** tab
3. Fill in:
   - **Your name**
   - **Outcome** (select one):
     - ‚ùå No Answer
     - üìû Voicemail
     - ‚úÖ Spoke with Contact
     - ‚ö†Ô∏è Wrong Number
     - üîÑ Callback Requested
     - üéâ Appointment Set
   - **Notes** (what happened, next steps)
4. Click **"Record Call Attempt"**

### 5. Schedule Appointment (If Successful!)

When you set an appointment:

1. **During the call**, confirm:
   - Date & time
   - Format (phone/in-person/video)
   - Who will attend
   
2. **In the system:**
   - Outcome: "Appointment Set"
   - Go to **"Appointment"** tab
   - Enter date, time, type
   - Add notes (location, attendees, agenda)
   - Click **"Schedule Appointment"**

3. **Confirmation:**
   - Status changes to **"Appointment Set"** (green badge)
   - Client is notified automatically
   - Your work is done for this lead! üéâ

## üö¶ Call Attempt Rules

### Timing
- **Minimum 2 days** between attempts
- **Maximum 6 attempts** per lead
- **14-day window** from when client sends email

### When to Stop
‚úã **Stop calling if:**
- Appointment is set (success!)
- Wrong number (mark as "Unqualified")
- Explicit "do not call" request
- 6 attempts reached

### Best Times to Call
- Tuesday - Thursday: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (local time)
- Avoid Mondays (busy) and Fridays (planning mode)

## üí° Call Outcomes Explained

| Outcome | What It Means | Next Step |
|---------|---------------|-----------|
| **No Answer** | Rang, no voicemail | Try again in 2+ days |
| **Voicemail** | Left message | Try again in 2+ days |
| **Spoke with Contact** | Had conversation | Try again or set appointment |
| **Wrong Number** | Invalid contact | Mark lead unqualified |
| **Callback Requested** | They want to be called back | Schedule callback, try in 2+ days |
| **Appointment Set** | SUCCESS! | Schedule in Appointment tab |

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**"I can't record a call attempt"**
- Check if 2 days have passed since last attempt
- Verify you haven't reached 6 attempts
- Make sure calling window hasn't expired (14 days)

**"The lead shows 'Max Attempts Reached'"**
- You've made all 6 calls - move to next lead
- Status is final, no more calls allowed

**"I set an appointment but can't save it"**
- Ensure date and time are filled in
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

**"Wrong contact information"**
- Select "Wrong Number" as outcome
- Add notes about what's wrong
- Lead will be marked "Unqualified"
